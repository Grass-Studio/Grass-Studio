Mountain Tales

Mountain Tales is an adventure game set in a vast world of towering mountains and wide plateaus, where harsh nature meets deep tranquility. The journey begins in a small, simple village, whose people live surrounded by legends and old tales.

The game emphasizes exploration, storytelling, and a meaningful journey. Every mountain tells a story, and every plateau hides a mystery. Adventure is about discovery, reflection, and living in a rich world with lore and choices.
