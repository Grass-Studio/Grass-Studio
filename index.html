About Us

This studio didn’t start with a big plan. It started with learning, curiosity, and small random games. Before COVID, I was just learning how to code. During COVID, Grass Studio was born—not as a business, but as an attempt to create something meaningful.

Most early games failed. None really took off. But I kept going. Years later, this studio is still here. No fixed genre. No limits. Just experiments, growth, and honest indie games. Still learning. Still building. Still trying.

Never lose hope. Never lose the flame.

Our Games

Mountain Tales

Mountain Tales is an adventure game set in a vast world of towering mountains and wide plateaus, where harsh nature meets deep tranquility. The journey begins in a small, simple village, whose people live surrounded by ancient legends and old tales passed down through generations—stories of heroes, mysterious creatures, and hidden forces dwelling in the high peaks.

The player embarks on mythical adventures focused on exploration, uncovering secrets, and facing challenges inspired by folklore and epic fantasy. Every mountain tells a story, every plateau hides a mystery, and as the journey progresses, deep connections between the village and the surrounding world are gradually revealed.

The game emphasizes atmosphere, storytelling, and the feeling of a slow, meaningful journey. Adventure is not just about combat, but about discovery, reflection, and living within a world rich with lore, choices, and consequences. Mountain Tales is not just a game—it is a story experienced step by step among the peaks.

Heartfall

Hony Life is a true open-world fantasy exploration game where the story is optional and freedom comes first.

Wander a petrified world shaped by a shattered crystal, uncover hidden locations, forgotten creatures, and mysteries at your own pace — or ignore the narrative entirely and simply explore.
